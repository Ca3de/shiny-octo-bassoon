Traumatic Brain Injury Waiver Services

Require a nursing-facility level of care, First Divine Touch can be part of the team that enables people to live comfortably at home. Under Indiana Medicaid's Traumatic Brain Injury Waiver (TBI), we send attendants to help with homemaker and housecleaning services as well as personal care, including meal prep, feeding, bathing, dressing, grooming, etc.

Indiana Medicaid Pays up to 100%

When retirees could use more help at home, Indiana Medicaid pays us to send helpers. The costs of this service are covered up to 100%. Please do not hesitate to contact us, learn more about eligibility, and get started with a Medicaid-paid caregiver.
